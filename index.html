Privacy Policy

The protection of your personal data is a fundamental concern for us. The IvyMental app was developed in accordance with the principle of "Privacy by Design" to guarantee you maximum privacy. Below we inform you in a simple and understandable way about how your data is processed.

In short: All data, voice inputs, analyses, and diary entries remain exclusively local on your mobile device. No data of any kind is transmitted to us or third parties.

1. Principle of Local Processing

All functions of the IvyMental app work fully offline on your device:

  • Voice inputs (Speech-to-Text): The transcription of your spoken diary entries is performed locally using the integrated Whisper model. No audio streaming to cloud services takes place.
  • Emotion analysis (ML): The determination of your mood and emotions is done via a local DistilBERT model directly on your device's processor.
  • Data storage: Your diary entries and analysis values are stored in a local, encrypted database (Hive) on your smartphone.

2. No Data Transmission & No Cloud

The app never sends data to external servers at any time. There is no registration, no user accounts, and no cloud synchronization. Your thoughts, feelings, and private recordings belong exclusively to you.

3. Exclusion of Analysis & Tracking Software

We do not use any analysis tools (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Flurry, or similar trackers) in our app. Your usage behavior is not recorded, not analyzed, and not processed further.

4. No Payment Functions or Third-Party Providers

The IvyMental app does not contain any in-app purchases, payment functions, or connections to payment service providers. No financial or billing data is collected.

5. Youth Protection & Content Restrictions

IvyMental is completely harmless under youth protection laws. Since the app has no social features, chats, or possibilities for interaction with other users, there is no risk of contact with inappropriate content or third parties.

6. No External File Access

The app does not access external files on your device. The required permissions are limited exclusively to the microphone (for local recording of your voice entries) and local notifications (for daily check-in reminders).

7. Your Rights (Data Deletion)

Since all your data is stored exclusively local on your device, you have full control:

  • You can delete individual entries at any time directly in the app.
  • You can delete all of the app's data at once by clearing the app data/cache of the IvyMental app in your smartphone's settings.
  • Upon uninstalling the app, all stored diary entries and analysis values are irretrievably deleted from your device.
